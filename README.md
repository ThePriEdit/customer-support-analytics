ğŸ” Key Insights
1. Billing is the biggest problem area

Billing-related tickets are the most frequent and also take the longest to handle.
This indicates:

Customer confusion around billing

Possible system/process inefficiencies

Need for improved agent training or self-service content

2. Phone support delivers the highest CSAT

Among all channels, Phone has the best customer satisfaction.
This suggests customers prefer:

Faster resolution

Human interaction

Real-time support

3. Billing issues have the highest handling time

This reinforces Billing as the primary operational challenge.

4. August 2024 saw the highest ticket volume

Seasonal/operational spikes should be considered for:

Workforce planning

Staffing levels

Load balancing

5. Escalation rate is high (36%)

This indicates:

First-contact resolution is low

Agents may lack decision-making authority

Processes need improvement
